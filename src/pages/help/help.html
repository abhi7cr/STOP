HOW TO USE THE APP: BASICS The STOP Constipation App is designed to help you practice sitting on the toilet on a regular basis, helping you poop on a more regular basis.
  1. Set Reminders: Set times for reminders to sit on the toilet and push for poop. Sitting after mealtimes makes use of the gastrocolic reflex which enables you to poop better after eating.
    • For Example: If you eat breakfast at 7 AM and Dinner at 6:30 PM you can set reminders to sit at these times.
  2. Set timer: You can set a timer for how long you sit on the toilet. Can be anywhere from between 1 minute and 10 minutes.
  3. Each day, when you receive the reminder to go sit on the toilet, you will be asked a series of questions:
    • Did you take your stool softener
    • Did you sit on the toilet
    • Did you produce stool
      • If the answer is no, you will register a smiley face on the calendar, and a message reminding you that the act of sitting is important and that the more you try to sit the stronger the colon gets
      • If the answer is yes, you will be asked to describe the stool. Patients will be able to slowly determine if their stools are going from a constipated form to a more regular form
      • If your answer is yes, you will receive a star (or poop emoji) on the calendar and an encouragement message, way to go. Regular sitting is the key to having you go to the bathroom regularly. Keep it up!
